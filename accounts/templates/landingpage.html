Frequently Asked Questions

What is a service subscription plan?

Viking Universe service subscription plans are regulated by a monthly hour limit of 160. The subscription plans are monthly agreements at promoted rates that will start the day that you purchase a service subscription plan, and will be charged to you for the same day. By subscribing to our services means you will have all the advantages of the benefits as promoted, and you can utilize the monthly hour limit according to your needs.

How does monthly recurring payment work?

Once you purchase a service subscription plan with us, you will be charged monthly; on the same day that you purchased unless you cancel it. For instance, your date of subscription is August 12, and then your next month's billing will be September 12 and so on.

What is included in my service subscription plan?

Every service subscription plan comes with flexible 160 hours of service from us. Remaining or unused hours for the subscribed month will be credited to the following month if you wish to continue your subscription.

What are the payment methods?

As of the moment, we only accept credit cards as a payment method for your convenience and safety. We will gladly notify you once we have updated the other methods of payment.

Is there any limit on a subscription per account?

There are no limits to our services. You can definitely have multiple subscriptions under one account. All you have to do is subscribe to the subscription plans of services that you wish to obtain.

Is my account transferable?

Your account is non-transferable. You cannot freely transfer the use of your subscription, however, you are allowed to purchase a specific service subscription plan for another person under your existing account.

What will happen to my subscription if my credit card is declined?

We will send a notification by email to inform you and we will give you 24 hours to resolve the issue. If the issue is not yet resolved, we will pause or terminate your subscription and reactivate it once your payment has successfully appeared.
